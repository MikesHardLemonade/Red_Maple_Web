About Us

Who are We?

Davis Red Maple Cultural Connection (DRMCC) is a non-profit or 501(c)3 organization based in Davis, California.

Mission:

The Mission of DRMCC is to promote awareness and understanding, support learning and communication, and nurture respect and appreciation among diverse cultures by providing dance, music, cultural, and language programs on individual and organizational levels.
